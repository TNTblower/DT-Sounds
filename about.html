About Us:

DT Sounds is a Combo between (DJ) Minix (15) and TNT333 (15). Both of them are Music Producer which a using the DAW FL Studio by Image Line. They met up at the age of 12 on a Discord Server. Both of them started to develope a friendship which resulted in them getting an idea to create sample packs under the now known name DT Sounds.
